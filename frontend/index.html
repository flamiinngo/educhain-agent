What we believe
Every child wants to learn. Not every child can afford to.
A parent who pulls their child from school is not giving up on education. They are choosing survival.
We do not blame them. We change the equation.
When learning becomes earning, the choice disappears. The child can do both.
This is not charity. The child earns because they proved they learned. That dignity is the whole point.

Three things that make
EduChain different

There are apps that teach. There are platforms that pay. EduChain is the first system that does both, autonomously, with every action permanently recorded on-chain.

No classroom. No teacher salary. No admin overhead. Just a child, a lesson, a quiz, and money sent the moment they pass.

01
The curriculum is structured,
not chosen.
A child does not pick what they feel like learning today. The agent knows their age, their grade level, and where they left off. It assigns the next lesson. Every child follows a real curriculum — the kind a school would deliver — because random learning is not education.
02
Every payment is on-chain
and verifiable.
The agent cannot lie. When it says a child was paid, there is a transaction hash on the Celo network to prove it. When it says a lesson was delivered, there is a permanent record on Filecoin. The receipts exist whether anyone checks them or not.
03
No human is needed for any of it.
The agent registers students, generates lessons, grades quizzes, detects fraud, sends payments, mints NFTs, and stores records. It does this around the clock. It does not take weekends off. It does not run out of motivation. It was built with one purpose and it pursues it without interruption.
